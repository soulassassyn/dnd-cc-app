Frequently Asked Questions

What is the D&D Backstory Generator?

The D&D Backstory Generator is a tool that can help you create an original and unique origin story for your character. All you need to do is give it your character's name, race and class and it can start creating amazing stories for you in no time. If you'd like a little more input, try out the personality quiz to help further define your character.

Not only is it a valuable resource for players but also for DMs to help flesh out the characters in your worlds, by providing an easy-to-use generator that can create a customized origin story that fits seamlessly into the campaign setting.

How do I use the D&D Backstory Generator?

After you create your account, just head over to Create Backstory and give it a try! You can have a couple paragraphs written about your character in no time by just providing some basic information.

How does it generate such incredible backstories?

The D&D Backstory Generator is designed to generate incredible backstories by using a combination of algorithms and curated content. The algorithm uses this information to create unique and compelling backstories based on the options you choose. Additionally, the AI is constantly being updated and tweaked so that it only improves over time.
